Subnetworks from the multilayer networks of author's keywords (AK) and KeyWords Plus keywords ( KP from Web of Knowlege). 
Every directory is named AK_KP_<n_nodes>_<id> where :
* <n_nodes>: is the number of nodes in the layers of AK and kP
* <id>: idenrificator of the subnetwork for a number of nodes.
